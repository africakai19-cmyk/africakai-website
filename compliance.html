Our Services

Everything Your Business Needs

From registration to ongoing compliance — we cover every regulatory obligation your business faces in South Africa.

01

Company Registration & Amendments

Get your business formally registered or update its structure with ease. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on building.

  • Private company (Pty) Ltd registrations
  • Director additions & removals
  • Shareholder amendments
  • Company name changes
  • Registered address changes
02

Compliance & Annual Returns

Stay in good standing with CIPC. Missing your annual return can result in deregistration — we make sure that never happens.

  • Annual returns submission (CIPC)
  • Compliance status checks
  • Ongoing CIPC filings
  • Beneficial ownership disclosures
  • Compliance status maintenance
03

Tax & SARS Services

From registration to compliance status — we handle all your SARS obligations efficiently and accurately.

  • Income tax registration
  • VAT registration
  • PAYE registration
  • Tax Compliance Status (TCS)
  • General SARS administrative support
04

Labour & Statutory Compliance

Ensure your business meets all employer obligations under South African labour law. Protect your business and your people.

  • UIF (Unemployment Insurance Fund) registration
  • COIDA registration
  • Department of Labour processes
  • Employer compliance support
05

Financial & Administrative Support

Beyond compliance, we help you maintain the financial records and documents that keep your business organised and audit-ready.

  • Basic bookkeeping assistance
  • Financial document preparation
  • Record keeping and document management
  • Business administrative support
